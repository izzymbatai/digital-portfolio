Core Story Set

Showed Leadership

LEEP Co-Lead Counselor

As a co-lead counselor for the Lakeside Educational Enrichment Program (LEEP), I was responsible for leading a team of 10+ counselors while supporting nearly 100 middle and high school students. One of my biggest challenges was organizing a multi-day camping trip for over 50 students. I coordinated logistics, ensured safety protocols, and delegated responsibilities across the team. I also worked to bridge communication between program administrators and counselors, often stepping in to handle overlooked or less desirable tasks to keep operations running smoothly. I consistently stayed late to make sure everything was prepared for the next day. As a result, the trip ran successfully, and the overall program experience was cohesive and impactful. I even created a highlight video that captured the program’s success and strengthened community among participants.

Worked with a Team

WashU Football

As a sophomore cornerback on the football team at Washington University in St. Louis, I work closely with over 100 players and coaches toward a common goal. Success depends on strong coordination both on and off the field, especially balancing demanding academic and athletic schedules. At my position, I’m part of the last line of defense, so I must fully understand our playbook and communicate effectively with teammates to ensure alignment. I often help others learn assignments and stay prepared. This shared accountability has contributed to our team’s success, including back-to-back 8-win seasons. Through this experience, I’ve learned that high-performing teams require trust, communication, and individual commitment to collective success.

Worked with Data

NBA TikTok Account

Over the past few years, I’ve built a TikTok account focused on NBA content, generating over 500,000 views. Much of my content involves player rankings and game predictions, which requires strong data analysis. To create credible and engaging content, I spend hours researching advanced stats, trends, and historical performance using sources like Basketball Reference and other analytics platforms. I evaluate which metrics truly impact team success and use that insight to shape my opinions. This process has sharpened my ability to interpret data and communicate insights clearly to an audience. As a result, my content has become more accurate and engaging, leading to higher viewership and audience trust. This experience taught me how to turn raw data into compelling, digestible narratives.

Gave a Presentation

ASA Fashion Show Emcee

I was selected as the emcee for the African Student Association’s 18th annual fashion show, the largest cultural event on campus, held in Graham Chapel. The role required months of preparation, as I worked on scripting to ensure the event was engaging, culturally respectful, and smoothly paced. I collaborated with multiple groups, including designers, dancers, and executive board members, to align messaging and timing across all segments. During the event, I presented live in front of a full audience, maintaining energy and adapting in real time to keep the show flowing. The event was a major success, with strong audience engagement throughout. This experience strengthened my public speaking skills and taught me how to confidently lead large-scale presentations while coordinating many moving parts behind the scenes.
